Assisted vs Non-Assisted Entry

Assisted Entry

  • Guided input with contextual help
  • Built-in validations and safeguards
  • Reduces errors for new staff

Non-Assisted Entry

  • Flexibility for experienced staff
  • Faster data entry for routine tasks

Decision Required: Preferred data entry approach - Assisted or Non-Assisted - to be adopted as the standard going forward.

Private Number Creation

  • Secure and controlled creation of private numbers
  • Clear audit trail – who created what, when, and why
  • Alignment with existing railway practices
  • Role-based permissions ensure proper authorization
  • Prevents unauthorized or duplicate entries
